Passive DNS

Phoenix is fed with passeive top level DNS traffic data. Therefore it is easily deployable, non-obtrusive and privacy preserving. The data is gathered by a SIE machine, then sent to our Vombato server (italian for wombat) where it is crunched and analyzed.

Botnets, clustered

Phoenix starts its analysis by separating AGDs from HGDs. Then it clusters the first depending on to which IPs they resolve to, possibly the C&Cs addresses. Final result? Clusters of malicious domains and their relative botmaster IP address. Voil√†!
